## Engagement 1 — Operational Dashboard Remediation, Mid-Market Distributor

**Client:** Confidential (industrial fasteners distributor, ~$12M revenue, 3 facilities)
**Scope:** Inherited a Vue 2 dashboard from a prior vendor with unresolved defects and no test coverage. Audited, remediated, and extended over a 10-week engagement.
**Relevance:** Near-identical situation to Meridian — thin handoff documentation, known-but-undocumented defects, IT team blocking changes. We established Playwright coverage before touching any production code; the client's IT team approved the first post-engagement change within a week of handoff.
**Outcome:** 11 defects resolved (9 were documented; 2 surfaced during audit), full test coverage across 5 views, architecture documentation delivered to internal IT.

## Engagement 2 — Inventory & Restocking Feature, Regional Manufacturer

**Client:** Confidential (precision parts manufacturer, EMEA operations)
**Scope:** Built a restocking recommendations module on top of an existing inventory system. Operator-supplied budget ceiling, ranked purchase order output, integration with existing ERP demand data.
**Relevance:** Direct precedent for R2. The recommendation logic — deficit detection, demand weighting, budget clipping — is the same pattern Meridian is asking for. We delivered it in 4 weeks as a standalone engagement.
**Outcome:** Feature adopted by operations team within the first week; reduced over-ordering by approximately 18% in the first quarter.

## Engagement 3 — Internationalization Rollout, APAC Warehouse Expansion

**Client:** Confidential (industrial components distributor, Tokyo warehouse opening)
**Scope:** Extended an English-only Vue application to support Japanese for warehouse staff. Partial i18n framework was already in place; we completed the rollout across 8 views.
**Relevance:** Direct precedent for D2. Same stack (Vue 3, existing vue-i18n setup), same driver (APAC warehouse staff with variable English proficiency).
**Outcome:** Tokyo team onboarded to the localized interface within 2 weeks of delivery; error rate on order entry dropped measurably in the first month.
